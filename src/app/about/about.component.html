FOCUS

CMDA Nigeria 5th Joint National Conference is the largest gathering of
medical and dental doctors and students in Nigeria. Thousands of
Christian Students, doctors and Health Professionals from Nigeria and
beyond join together for four days to learn and share ideas. The
conference format strives to educate, inspire, encourage, connect and
equip attendees.

It provides a unique platform to mobilize, engage, envision, and equip
over 2,000 medics that have the capacity to bring about a paradigm shift
in the Health System in Nigeria and beyond with the potential to deliver
an efficient, effective and responsive health service.
Attendance will include medical and dental doctors and students from
all geopolitical zones in Nigeria as well as from diaspora.

WHAT TO EXPECT ?

Inspiring talks from keynote speakers who are leading practitioners in the health sector
Plenary and parallel sessions in key areas of the health landscape
Times of worship and corporate prayer
Opportunities to network with Like minds
Scientific Exhibitions
Panel sessions and lots more!
