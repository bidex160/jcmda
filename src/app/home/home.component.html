You can register to attend the 5JNC/50th Anniversary event virtually if you cannot do so physically. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What should I do now ?

Click on the Register button, select registration category, fill the registration form, make payment and prepare to be blessed. Upon payment, a receipt will be sent to your registered email. At any time before the conference, your login details grants you access to your dashboard. This enables you to access information on accommodation and transportation, as well as to order conference materials.

Does Conference offer CME (CPD) Points ?

Yes the continuing medical education lectures and sessions in the conference will offer 10 CPD Points to all registered participants

Is there any discount for couple participants ?

Yes, there is a discount. Select couple under registration.

Can I get media materials ?

Yes, you can. From our website and social media pages.

How early can I get media materials ?

Media materials are updated from time to time. As early as 6 months to the conference, they are available.

How much refund do I get if I cancel my participation after registering ?

Check our refund policy.

Can I register during the conference ?

Yes you can but this would attract late registration fees and would make our planning for you more challenging

Can I get my organization sponsor my attendance ?

Yes, you can get your organisation to sponsor your attendance. Simply select sponsored by organisation during registration process.

Is there provision for lodging ?

Yes, there is. Accommodation will be available 3 months before the conference.
